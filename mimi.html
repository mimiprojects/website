About

I am a content manager, researcher, editor and terrible troubleshooter from Toronto. I started my career in magazine and web publishing, first as an intern and fact checker at Toronto Life magazine, and later became the online listings editor of torontolife.com and where.ca. Currently, I am a librarian in the film group at the Ontario Media Development Corporation. I hold a Bachelor of Journalism degree in Magazine Journalism from Ryerson University and a Master of Information Studies in Library and Information Science from University of Toronto.

Portfolio

Here are a couple of things I have worked on:

Film Locations Database

The Digitial Location Library is a database containing more than 9,000 listings and 250,000 photos of locations in Ontario to assist film and TV producers in finding places to shoot their project. For the pre-production stage of filming, the database is a key research tool in selecting the perfect real setting to match a scene in a feature film, TV series, documentary, commerical or short.

Online Listings

Toronto Life magazine's website, torontolife.com, features listings and reviews of more than 1,400 local restaurants, bars & clubs and food shops. I managed the listings as well as worked with editors, fact checkers and developers on a redesign project to relaunch three databases and expand Toronto Life's online Eating & Drinking Guide.
